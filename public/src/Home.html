About the Game Bumper Tanks is a drive around fun bumping shooting game. The Goal of the game is to stand up until the last among all your friends.

Have real-time weather experience inside the game with the cool weather themes. Pick power ups to have extra health, heavy attacks and increase spead.

Design your own tanks, with different type of guns that have their own speciality.

Be a Strategist!! Make use of the physics and kill enemies hidden in corners of the map.
